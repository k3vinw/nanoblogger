Example Article

<p>
This is an example article. The articles plugin provides a simple way for NanoBlogger to automatically generate and update articles for your weblog. Creating a new article is easy, create a new file in the <em>articles</em> directory and save it with the suffix, "txt". To remove an article simply rename it's suffix to something other than "txt" or remove it from the <em>articles</em> directory.
</p>
